Why Circle Boats?

A New Way to Experience the Gulf

Our portable inflatable circle boats combine fun, safety, and sustainability into one unforgettable experience.

⚡

Electric & Eco-Friendly

Powered by 35lb thrust electric trolling motors. Zero emissions, zero noise pollution — just clean, quiet fun.

💪

Stable & Safe

Inflatable design with a low center of gravity. Incredibly stable even in choppy water. Life jackets provided.

🛶

Portable Design

Our boats are inflatable and portable. We can bring the experience to your event, party, or private dock.

🏋

No License Needed

Anyone can drive a circle boat. No boating license, no experience required. We teach you in 5 minutes.

🌊

Gulf Coast Views

Explore the beautiful Orange Beach coastline, intercoastal waterways, and Gulf Islands from a whole new perspective.

🎉

Perfect for Events

Birthday parties, date nights, bachelor/bachelorette parties, team outings — circle boats make any event special.
